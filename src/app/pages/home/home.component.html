Frequently Asked Questions

+
1. What services are provided by a Company Secretary?

A Company Secretary provides comprehensive corporate compliance and advisory services, including company incorporation, ROC filings, annual compliances, board and shareholder meeting management, drafting of legal resolutions, maintenance of statutory registers, corporate governance advisory, regulatory filings, and secretarial audits.

+
2. Why does a company need a Company Secretary?

A Company Secretary ensures that a company complies with the provisions of the Companies Act, SEBI regulations (where applicable), and other corporate laws. This helps the company avoid penalties, strengthen governance, and maintain transparency with stakeholders.

+
3. What is included in annual compliance services?

Annual compliance typically includes:

  • Preparation and filing of annual forms with the Registrar of Companies
  • Drafting of Directors’ Report and statutory disclosures
  • Convening and documenting Board and Annual General Meetings
  • Maintenance of statutory registers and records
  • Filing of event-based compliances
+
4. Can you assist with company incorporation?

Yes. Company Secretaries provide end-to-end support for company incorporation, including name approval, drafting of MOA and AOA, digital signature procurement, DIN services, and filing of incorporation documents with the Registrar of Companies.

+
5. Do you provide services for private, public, and listed companies?

Yes. Services are provided to private limited companies, public limited companies, unlisted public companies, listed companies, LLPs, startups, and other corporate entities in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

+
6. What are secretarial audit services?

Secretarial audit is a compliance audit conducted to evaluate whether a company has complied with applicable corporate laws and secretarial standards. It helps in identifying gaps, risks, and areas of improvement in corporate governance practices.

+
7. Can you handle SEBI and stock exchange compliances?

Yes. Support is provided for SEBI regulations and stock exchange compliances, including listing obligations, disclosures, corporate governance reporting, and periodic filings for listed entities.

+
8. Do you assist with funding, IPOs, and corporate restructuring?

Yes. Company Secretaries assist with private equity funding, venture capital transactions, IPOs, rights issues, preferential allotments, mergers, demergers, and other restructuring exercises.

+
9. What documents are required to start CS services?

Typically, the following documents are required:

  • Certificate of Incorporation
  • PAN and TAN
  • MOA and AOA
  • List of Directors and Shareholders
  • Previous compliance records (if applicable)
+
10. How are professional fees determined?

Professional fees are based on the scope of work, complexity of services, frequency of filings, and the size and regulatory structure of the organization. Transparent proposals are provided before commencement of services.
